Portfolio

Enlight.info News Portal

Project Description

The project aimed to create a feature-rich news portal, drawing inspiration from the BBC website's design and functionality. The objective was to develop a dynamic platform that delivers news content effectively, provides an exceptional user experience, and offers seamless navigation.

Details about the Project:

Project Scope:
This project encompassed the entire development process of a responsive news website, including real-time news updates, multimedia integration, user interaction features, and a user-friendly interface.
    Technologies Used:
  1. Front-end: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and jQuery for structuring, styling, and enhancing the website's interactivity.
  2. Back-end: Utilized PHP for server-side scripting and MySQL for managing the database.
  3. Content Management System (CMS): Integrated a custom CMS to empower news editors with efficient article management capabilities.
  4. Responsive Design: Implemented responsive web design principles to ensure seamless user experiences on various devices and screen sizes.

Outcomes:

  • A visually engaging news portal inspired by the BBC's design philosophy.
  • Real-time news updates and a well-organized news section for easy exploration.
  • Rich multimedia integration, including image galleries, video players, and audio features.
  • Interactive commenting system, facilitating user engagement and discussions.
  • A user-friendly content management system, enabling news editors to streamline article publication and updates.
  • Social media integration to extend the website's reach and audience engagement.
  • Enhanced website performance, with optimized load times and efficient content delivery.
